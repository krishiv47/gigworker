GigGuard

PASSIVE CRASH DETECTION → AMBULANCE DISPATCH
AI Offline

A safety net for gig & delivery riders. The driver app silently watches the phone's motion sensors; the instant it detects a crash it auto-sends an SOS with location and an AI injury report. The dispatch centre sees it live on a map and sends the nearest ambulance — no buttons, no fumbling, even if the rider is unconscious.

Driver App

Runs on the rider's phone. Detects impacts from the accelerometer, gives a 15-second "I'm OK" cancel window, then auto-dispatches an SOS with GPS + sensor data.

Open driver app →

Dispatch Center

Mission control for operators. Live incident feed, map tracking, AI medical assessment, and one-click ambulance dispatch with real-time ETA.

Open dispatch center →

AI Triage

Real-time injury classification powered by AI. Analyzes crash telemetry to assess severity, likely injuries, and recommended dispatch type.

Auto Narrative

AI generates plain-English incident summaries so dispatch operators understand the situation at a glance.

Smart First-Aid

Context-specific first-aid instructions generated by AI that dispatch can relay to bystanders on scene.

Operator Chat

Dispatch operators can chat with AI about any incident — ask about injuries, treatment, or dispatch strategy.

AI Settings

Disconnected
Groq AI is active by default — calls run through a secure server proxy, so the API key never reaches the browser. Triage, narratives and chat work out of the box.
